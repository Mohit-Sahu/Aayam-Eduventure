Entrance Exams

JEE Main – Joint Entrance Examination Main

Joint Entrance Examination - Main or commonly known as JEE Main is a national level entrance exam conducted by NTA to offer admission to BE/BTech, BPlan and BArch courses at the IITs (Indian Institute of Information Technology), NITs (National Institute of Technology), NITs (National Institute of Technology) and other Centrally Funded Technical Institutions (CFTIs) across the country. Only the top 2.5 lakh score holders in JEE Main 2020 will be eligible to apply for JEE Advanced.

JEE Advanced – Joint Entrance Examination Advance

JEE Advanced is the entrance examination conducted by the seven zonal Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) under the guidance of Joint Admission Board (JAB). It is a national level examination through which admission to B.Tech, Integrated M.Tech or B.Tech-M.Tech Dual Degree in Engineering, Architecture, Science & Pharmacy is provided in 23 IITs. This examination is open for Indian national as well as for foreign national candidates. JEE Advanced is the second stage of Joint Entrance Examination (JEE)

BITSAT – Birla Institute of Technology & Science Admission Test

BITSAT is the admission test for the on-campus degree progarmmes offered at BITS Pilani campus, Goa Campus and Hyderabad Campus. Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS), Pilani a leading Institute of Higher Education and a deemed University under section 3 of the UGC act offers degree programmes in Engineering, Sciences, Technology, Pharmacy, Management and Humanities.


COURSES WE OFFER


Lakshya (Two Year Regular Programme)

(For XI Students)

This course gives a good head start to all those students who aspire for engineering / medical as a career. It also builds a very sound base for class XI school exams.

The course is divided into two academic sessions:
Academic Session-1 : The syllabus of XI class is covered along with the preparation of JEE as well as other competitive exam. Regular tests are conducted on JEE (Main & Advanced) / Medical pattern. Entire course of class XI is completed by January and students are let off till 3rd week of March, for their XI class final exams.
Academic Session-2: The syllabus of class XII is taught thoroughly for board exams as well as for JEE (Main & Advanced) and Medical. Students are grilled through regular test’s on JEE & other competitive exams.
Additional Features
  • The study material / DPP will be given to the students.
  • The result of every test shall be made available in the institute notice board/email/ sms to parents.
  • After the completion of class XII Syllabus, a special revision test series is conducted for further revision & strengthen the class XI Syllabus taught earlier.
